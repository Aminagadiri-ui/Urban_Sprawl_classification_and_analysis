Key Findings & Recommendations

Scientific conclusions from 25 years of satellite data

Five Key Scientific Findings
Finding 01
Urban expansion confirmed across all four cities (2000–2025)
All study cities show positive urban growth over the 25-year period. Bangkok and Mexico City show the strongest absolute area increases. Paris shows the smallest relative change, consistent with its mature urban structure and strong planning framework (PLU zoning).
Finding 02
2015 Landsat anomaly was a pipeline artefact, not genuine urban contraction
Atlanta's 2015 V-shaped dip was traced to winter compositing allowing Dilated Cloud (Bit 1) halos into the composite. Two-layer fix: seasonal compositing + anomaly interpolation. This fully corrects all reported metrics.
Finding 03
Cross-domain accuracy is climate-zone dependent
Paris achieves highest Urban F1-scores (0.72–0.82), benefiting from strong spectral contrast. Bangkok and Atlanta show lower F1 (0.45–0.72) due to tropical forest canopy confusion and low-density suburban sprawl.
Finding 04
Land consumption outpaces population growth in most city-periods
Urban land expanded faster than urban population for the majority of city-period combinations. Atlanta shows the most persistent sprawl (LCR > 1.5). Paris shows the most balanced recent performance (LCR approaching 1.0 post-2015).
Finding 05
Urban morphology diverges systematically across climate zones
Compactness metrics confirm Bangkok and Mexico City develop more compactly (lower LSI, higher LPI, lower PD) than Atlanta, reflecting the difference between state-guided corridor development and market-driven dispersed sprawl.
Recommendations
Data & Model
Add multi-city training data to improve model generalisation
Use texture features and Sentinel-1 SAR for better urban detection
Use higher-resolution imagery (Sentinel-2) for finer boundaries
Validation & Extension
Validate results using OSM, GHSL, and building footprints
Extend analysis to district-level and predictive urban growth modelling
Link outputs to SDG indicators and environmental impact assessment
Dissemination
Publish code and data for full reproducibility
Develop a GIS dashboard for operational urban planning use
